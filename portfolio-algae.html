Smart Lake Monitoring: Detecting Algae with AI

Project Info

  • Category: Computer Vision / ML
  • Tech Stack: Python, CNN (TensorFlow)
  • Status: Research Project
  • Date: 2023 - Present

Moving from 'Color-based Statistical Analysis' to 'Vision-based Deep Learning'.

Detailed Story

The Challenge: Traditional water quality testing involves sending boats to collect samples and analyzing them in labs—a slow and expensive process. My goal was to act as an "optometrist" for lakes, determining water health from a single photo.

Why CNN? Unlike simple color analysis, the CNN mimics the human eye. It identifies texture patterns—like paint-like scum or granular clumps specific to Harmful Algal Blooms (HABs). This allows the model to distinguish between "green water" caused by algae and "green reflections" from trees.

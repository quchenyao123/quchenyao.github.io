Detailed Story

Analyzed complex gene expression data to classify cancer types.

Challenge: Gene data is typically "Small N, Large P" (few patients, thousands of genes), which breaks traditional statistical models.

Solution: Applied advanced dimension reduction techniques and regularized regression methods to isolate significant gene markers associated with specific cancer subtypes, enabling accurate diagnosis from genetic profiles.

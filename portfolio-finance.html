Detailed Story

This project focused on predicting company valuation using high-dimensional financial data.

Key Techniques: I utilized Lasso Regression (Least Absolute Shrinkage and Selection Operator) to handle multicollinearity and perform feature selection. By shrinking less important coefficients to zero, the model identified the most critical financial indicators driving company value, creating a robust and interpretable prediction model.

Detailed Story

In this project, I utilized statistical learning methods to build a valuation model for companies to predict their market value accurately.

The Challenge: Financial datasets often contain hundreds of indicators that are highly correlated (multicollinearity), which confuses traditional regression models.

Methodology: Using R Language, I applied Lasso Regression. Lasso is powerful because it not only predicts values but also performs feature selectionâ€”automatically "shrinking" the coefficients of less important variables to zero. This allowed the model to identify the true key drivers of company value.
